SEC Football Canvas Game

How It Works

  1. Signup and pick your favorite team
  2. Pick home and away teams
  3. Away team selects Heads or Tails
  4. Winner of the toss gets the ball first
  5. Click the player buttons to make plays
  6. Yards are a function of player strength, speed, and energy

Things to Know

  • The first three player buttons are run plays
  • The last three player buttons are pass plays
  • Punt and field goal options only occur on 4th down
  • You will always make field goals inside the 20 yard line
  • Players gain energy on every play they are not used
  • Turnovers happen 3 out of every 100 plays on average
